## Why this exists

Most people use ChatGPT like a smarter Google. They type a question, skim the answer, close the tab. That extracts maybe 10% of what the tool can do for a knowledge worker.

The gap is not intelligence or access — it is operator fluency. Knowing when to role-prompt, how to iterate instead of re-ask, when to switch models, how to chain outputs, when to stop and rewrite the instruction. A one-off €249 session flattens the learning curve that otherwise takes a year of quiet frustration.

Everything you leave with — the prompt playbook, the cheatsheet — runs offline, on your machine, in any text editor. No app, no subscription, no cloud. The system is yours the moment the call ends.

## Who it's for

- Founders, PMs, analysts — operators who don't want a fluff course
- People who've tried ChatGPT, hit the wall, and want their prompts to actually work
- Teams who need one person fluent enough to make the rest fluent
- Anyone tired of paying a subscription for a model they don't know how to drive

## What you leave with

**Make ChatGPT Your Slave** is a productized 90-minute 1:1 engagement. You do not buy a workshop — you buy an operating system for the tool.

The spine:

- **Pre-session intake** — you send me two or three real weekly workflows (writing, research, review, planning) ahead of the call.
- **Live rebuild** — on the call, we rebuild those workflows together. You watch the iteration happen on the first one; you drive the second half.
- **Named methodology** — role-framing, iteration loops, output chaining, model routing (ChatGPT vs Claude vs local). Every move is named so you can repeat it.
- **Playbook handoff** — you leave with a custom prompt playbook and a one-page model-routing cheatsheet, both tailored to the workflows we rebuilt.
- **Async follow-up** — two weeks of written-only unblocking after the call. Stuck on a prompt? Send it. I reply within a working day.
