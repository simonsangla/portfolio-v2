The Revenue Root Cause Engine engagement lands between **€15,000 and €25,000**, fixed before kickoff. Three things move the number inside that band:

1. **Data complexity** — how many product surfaces, cohorts, and channels contribute to the KPI.
2. **Event schema cleanliness** — a tidy dbt-modelled layer costs less than raw Segment events that still need de-duplication.
3. **Timeline pressure** — the default is four weeks. Compressed timelines (two-week sprints) sit at the top of the band.

The fractional engagement runs **€8–12K per month** depending on cadence (2 vs 3 days per week) and commitment length (3 vs 6 months). The ChatGPT training is **€249**, flat.

I don't run below €15K for the engagement. Below that, the diagnostic ends up rushed, the Streamlit tool ends up thin, and you end up with an artifact your team won't trust. If budget is tight and the problem is narrow, the training session or a one-off prompt-optimizer deployment is the honest answer.
