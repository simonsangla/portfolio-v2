You'll hear from me within **48 hours**, in writing.

Step one is a **scoped written proposal**: the KPI target, the data we'll need access to, the decomposition plan, the deliverables, the fixed price, and the start date. No slide deck, no "discovery call" — a one-page document you can forward internally.

If the scope fits, you reply yes. We agree in writing, I send a short SOW, you countersign, and we pick a kickoff date. The whole pre-engagement loop usually closes in under a week.

If your situation isn't a fit — wrong data stack, wrong scale, wrong problem shape — I'll say so plainly in the reply, name what a better fit looks like, and suggest an alternative. I'd rather lose a bad-fit engagement in the first exchange than drag both of us through a mismatched four weeks.

No phone calls, no automated follow-ups, no CRM drip.
