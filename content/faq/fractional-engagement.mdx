The cadence is deliberately async. We **kick off with one working call** to align on priorities, scope, data access, and the first diagnostic target. After that, the week looks like this: I work on your data and artifacts 2–3 days per week, you get **async updates via Slack or shared docs**, and we hold **one 1-hour weekly status check** — written brief sent ahead, call used only to resolve the 2–3 decisions it surfaces.

Typical engagement runs 3–6 months. Retainer is €8–12K per month depending on cadence, fixed in writing before kickoff. No auto-renewal — at wrap, your analyst owns the framework and tools.

The point of async isn't cost-cutting; it's respect for your team's deep work and a searchable record of every decision instead of untraceable meeting drift.
