Four concrete things happen inside the 90-minute session:

1. **Live diagnostic** of your current prompts. We open your real ChatGPT history together. I show you where the model is ignoring half your instructions and why.
2. **A reusable prompt scaffold** — role, context, constraints, output spec. You learn to compose, not copy-paste. No more blank-page prompt anxiety.
3. **Three weekly workflows rebuilt on the call.** You pick three real tasks you do every week. We rebuild them as ChatGPT workflows during the session. You walk out with them running.
4. **A one-page cheat-sheet** of the patterns that matter — yours to keep, pinned above your monitor.

After the call, you have **two weeks of async follow-up**: stuck on a prompt, send it, I reply within a working day. Fourteen days of unblocking included at no extra cost.

One video call, written-only afterward. No subscription, no upsell.
