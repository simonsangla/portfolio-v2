Your data stays in **your** warehouse. I never copy it out.

For the Revenue Root Cause Engine and fractional engagements, I work via **read-only Snowflake access** against your account. Every query, every model, every Streamlit panel executes inside your environment — no extract, no staging bucket on my side, no cloud "workspace" that holds your rows. When the engagement wraps, revoke my role and nothing about your data leaves with me.

For the ChatGPT training, nothing sensitive goes to a model — we work on your prompts' structure, not on your real production data.

Products I ship publicly (Prompt Optimizer, Dual Pronto, Theme Forge) are explicitly **local-only / bring-your-own-key**: the app runs in your browser, your inputs hit the LLM API with your own key, and no data round-trips through my servers. That's what the "100% offline · local-only · no data leak" line under the hero refers to.
