Principles

How to write for this site

Five rules. Nothing else.

  • One verbLead with a verb. Skim-first audience. "Start the intake" beats "Begin your journey with us today".
  • Numbers winConcrete beats adjective. "€15–25K, 4 weeks" not "competitive pricing, flexible timelines".
  • Written recordEvery claim has a paper trail. If you can't point at the repo, the MDX, or the dashboard, don't claim it.
  • No callsAsync is the product. Copy never invites a call — it invites an intake, a brief, or a Loom.
  • No exclamationAnd no emoji. Energy comes from specificity, not punctuation.
