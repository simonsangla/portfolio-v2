Principles

  • One familyInter from 15px captions through the 56px hero h1. No serif, no Fraunces anywhere.
  • Weight = hierarchy400 body · 500 section headings · 600 only the hero h1. Never bold anywhere else.
  • MonoEyebrows, meta rows, numeric labels, code. Never body, never a tagline body fallback.
  • DisambiguationInter loaded with ss01, cv11 — open a/g, upright R/J, distinct 1/l/I/0.
  • ItalicsNever. Emphasis comes from weight, mono, or colour.
  • MeasureProse cap at 42rem (~65ch). Hero widens from 640px to 960px at lg.
